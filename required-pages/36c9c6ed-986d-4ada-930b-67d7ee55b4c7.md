The My Links feature lets you pin pages to the sidebar for quick access.

## Description

When you are signed in, any page can be bookmarked to your personal **My Links** list. Pinned pages appear in the sidebar under a "My Links" heading. The list scrolls independently if you have many entries (up to 20).

My Links are personal — each user has their own list and changes do not affect other users.

## Adding a Page

Open the **More** menu (the `⋯` or gear icon in the page toolbar) and choose **Add to My Links**. The page is immediately added to your sidebar and the menu entry changes to **Remove from My Links**.

## Removing a Page

To remove a pinned page, either:

- Open the **More** menu on that page and choose **Remove from My Links**, or
- Click the **×** button next to the link in the sidebar.

Both methods take effect immediately without a full page reload.

## Notes

- You must be signed in — anonymous users do not see My Links.
- A maximum of 20 pages can be pinned.
- Pinned page titles reflect the title at the time you pinned the page. If a page is renamed, use Remove then Add again to update the label.
