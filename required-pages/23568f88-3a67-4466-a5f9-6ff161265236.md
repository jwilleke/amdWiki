# Privacy Notice

This notice explains what information the platform collects during your session and how it is used. For a live view of the data currently collected about your session, see [Everything We Know About You|/view/Everything%20We%20Know%20About%20You].

## Information We Collect

During your session we collect the following types of information:

- **User Profile Data**: Your username, display name, email, and roles
- **User Preferences**: Your saved display and editor preferences (see below)
- **Session Data**: A temporary session identifier maintained for the duration of your visit
- **Browser Information**: Your browser type, user agent string, and accepted languages
- **Network Data**: Your client IP address and referrer URL
- **System Data**: Server-side application state used to generate page content

## User Preferences Stored

The following preferences are stored as part of your user profile and are used to personalize your experience:

%%table-striped
|| Preference || Stored As || Description ||
| **Light / Dark Mode** | `display.theme` | Your display brightness preference: light, dark, or system default |
| **Site Theme** | `ngdpbase.theme.active` | The Bootstrap/Bootswatch theme applied to the site |
| **Code Editor Style** | `editor.theme` | Your syntax-highlighting scheme for the page editor textarea only |
| **Locale** | `preferences.locale` | Your preferred language and regional format |
| **Timezone** | `preferences.timezone` | Your preferred timezone for date/time display |
| **Date Format** | `preferences.dateFormat` | Your preferred date display format |
/%

You can view and change all preferences at any time on your [User Profile|/profile] page.

## Data Usage

- **User Profile Data**: Used for authentication and personalization
- **User Preferences**: Used to apply your chosen display settings and editor configuration on each page load
- **Session Data**: Maintained temporarily for your session only; discarded when the session expires or you log out
- **Browser Information**: Used for compatibility checking and security
- **Network Data**: Used for security monitoring and access control
- **System Data**: Used for performance monitoring and statistics

## Data Retention

- **Session Data**: Deleted when your session expires or you log out
- **User Profile & Preferences**: Retained as long as your account is active
- **Log Data**: Retained per the system log retention policy configured by the administrator

## Your Rights

You may view and update your personal information and preferences at any time on your [User Profile|/profile] page.

To request deletion of your account or data, contact your administrator.
