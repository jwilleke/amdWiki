[{$pagename}] are [Platform Variables] that are based on your current context within [{$applicationname}].

[{$pagename}] are  [Platform Variables] that require __user or page__ context Like:

- what page you are on: [{$pagename}]
- Your User Name: [{$username}]
- etc.

[{$pagename}] are provided from [Plugins]
