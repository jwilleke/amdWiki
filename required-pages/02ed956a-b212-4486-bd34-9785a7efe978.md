# Profile Page

Every user in [{$applicationname}] has a **Profile Page** — a personal page automatically created when your account is set up. Your profile page is named **Profile: Your Name** and is accessible at `/view/profile-your-name`.

## Who Can See Profile Pages

Profile pages are visible to **any authenticated user** — meaning anyone who is logged in, including Occupants. Anonymous (not logged in) users cannot view profile pages.

%%table-striped
|| Role || Can View || Can Edit ||
| Admin | Yes | Yes |
| Editor | Yes | No — author-locked |
| Contributor | Yes | No — author-locked |
| Reader | Yes | No — author-locked |
| Occupant | Yes | No — author-locked |
| Anonymous | No | No |
/%

The page author themselves can always edit their own profile page regardless of role.

## What Is a Profile Page?

Your profile page is your personal space on the platform. Out of the box it includes sections for:

- **About Me** — a brief introduction, areas of expertise, or anything you want others to know
- **My Contributions** — pages you have created or edited
- **My Projects** — areas of focus or ongoing work
- **Contact Information** — how others can reach you
- **Links and References** — useful resources you want to share

You can edit your profile page at any time to keep it current.

## Finding Your Profile Page

- Click your name or avatar in the top navigation bar and choose **Profile**
- Go directly to `/view/profile-your-name` (e.g. `/view/profile-jim-willeke`)
- Use the search bar and search for your name

## Editing Your Profile Page

Your profile page is **author-locked** — only you and administrators can edit it. Other authenticated users can view it but cannot modify it.

To edit your profile:

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click the **Edit** button in the page toolbar
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

## Author Lock

The **Author Lock** on your profile page means:

- Only the page author (you) and administrators can open the edit form
- Other users — even editors — are blocked from editing it
- The lock can only be set or removed by an administrator

This protects your profile from being modified by others.

## Profile Page vs. the About You Page

%%table-striped
||  || Profile Page || [About You || /view/about-you] ||
| **Purpose** | Your personal page | System info about your current session |
| **Editable** | Yes — by you and admins | No — auto-generated |
| **Content** | What you choose to share | Session, browser, and user data |
/%
