# Page History

**Page History** shows the revision history of the current page. It is available from the **Info** dropdown in the toolbar.

Clicking **Page History** navigates to `/history/PageName`, which lists all saved versions of the page in reverse chronological order.

## What It Shows

Each entry in the history list includes the version number, the date and time of the save, and the user who made the change.
