# System Category

The **System Category** classifies a page by its role in [{$applicationname}]. It controls where the page is stored and how the system treats it.

## Available Categories

[{ConfigAccessor type='systemCategories'}]

## Who Can Change It

Only **administrators** can change the System Category. Non-admin users see it as a read-only field.

## Why It Matters

The category determines the page's `storageLocation`. Pages in the `documentation` and `system` categories are managed as **required pages** — they are seeded from the application source and can be synced by an admin. Pages in `general` and `addon` are regular content.

Changing the category of an existing page may affect whether it appears in Required Pages Sync and how it is treated during upgrades.
