# Page Title

The **Page Title** is the name of the page. It appears at the top of the page, in search results, in navigation menus, and forms the page's URL.

## Rules

- The title must be **unique** across [{$applicationname}] — no two pages can share the same title.
- The following characters are **not allowed**: `/ \ # ? % " ' < > | *`
- Titles are **case-sensitive** for storage but matched case-insensitively in links.
- Spaces are allowed and are preserved in the title.

## How the Title Becomes a URL

The page title is used directly in the URL. For example, a page titled **My First Page** is accessed at `/view/My First Page`. Special characters in the URL are encoded automatically.

## Changing the Title

Saving a page with a changed title creates the page at the new title. The old title is not automatically redirected — any existing links to the old title will break. Update links manually after renaming a page.
