# Make a Reservation

Use this page to request time on a reservation calendar (Clubhouse, Tennis Court, etc.).

You must be **logged in** to submit a reservation.

---

## Before You Submit

- Check the [Calendar](Calendar) to confirm your desired time slot is available.
- Reservations are confirmed on a first-come, first-served basis.
- You will receive an email confirmation at your account email address.
- The facility manager is notified automatically.

---

## Reservation Form

[{ReservationFormPlugin}]

If the form does not appear, ensure you are logged in and that the Calendar add-on is enabled.

---

## Cancellations

To cancel a reservation you have made, contact your site administrator and provide:

- Your name
- The calendar (e.g. Clubhouse)
- The date and time of the reservation

---

## Policies

- Reservations must be made at least **24 hours** in advance.
- Back-to-back reservations by the same member require manager approval.
- Please leave the facility in the condition you found it.
